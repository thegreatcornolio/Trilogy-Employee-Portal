🇿🇦 June 16 — Youth Day
June is Youth Month in South Africa — a time to honour the extraordinary courage of young people who stood up and changed the course of history. On June 16, 1976, thousands of students in Soweto took to the streets to protest against the apartheid government's imposition of Afrikaans as the language of instruction in Black schools. What followed was one of the most significant and painful turning points in our struggle for liberation.
The Soweto Uprising galvanised a generation and drew the world's attention to the brutality of apartheid. After democracy was established in 1994, June was declared Youth Month and June 16 became National Youth Day — a permanent reminder that young people have the power to change the world.
While many of the Trilogy team enjoy a day off, our NADClinic team will be at their desks, delivering for our client. That is not a small thing — it is a big thing, and it deserves to be said clearly: thank you. While the rest of South Africa pauses to honour this day, you are showing up, delivering, and representing Trilogy with everything you have. I see you. Leadership sees you. And we are incredibly grateful. 💚

🤝 A landmark partnership — Trilogy Digital
We have concluded a partnership between DSG / CXG and TrilogyBPO — combining enterprise CX delivery with AI-powered BPO capability for global brands. Together, we are launching Trilogy Digital — a joint venture with an ambition to grow to 300 seats over the next couple of years.
DSG is South Africa's leading customer experience and technology group — seven specialist businesses delivering contact centre operations, marketing, telecoms, managed security and commerce solutions. CXG, a DSG company, is their specialist BPO and CX delivery arm with 27 years of expertise across telecoms, retail, entertainment and financial services, powered by the QContact UCaaS platform — which will become our communications tool.
Trilogy Digital brings together two organisations that have spent decades at the sharp end of enterprise customer experience to create a truly digital-first experience. This partnership gives us access to specialist UCaaS resources, operational support, and best-in-class CX tools as we grow.
